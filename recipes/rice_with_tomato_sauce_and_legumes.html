Rice with tomato sauce and legumes

Hearty meal to start a day

This quick dish brings flavour and texture to my otherwise simple diet. It's rich in complex carbohydrates and micronutrients. To top it off I add handful herbs and spices for beneficial antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • (100 g) White rice parboiled
  • (100 g) Tomato sauce "Muti" brand
  • (70 g) Canned corn
  • (60 g) Canned kidney beans
  • (Single) Large egg
  • (To your liking) Turmeric, pepper, garlic powder, fenugreek seed

Instructions:

  1. Start boiling rice.
  2. Measure and mix together: tomato sauce, corn, kidney beans and spices. Leave the egg for later.
  3. Spread touch of cooking oil on the pan and heat it up. Add the tomato sauce mixture.
  4. Gently stir the sauce from time to time. After 5 minutes of cooking break the egg into the pan. Keep stirring.
  5. Soon after breaking the egg, the rice should be ready. Drain it off and throw into the pan.
  6. Mix it all. Enjoy!
