Rice with tomato sauce and legumes

Hearty meal to start a day

This quick dish brings flavour and texture to my otherwise simple diet. It's rich in complex carbohydrates and micronutrients. To top it off I add handful herbs and spices for beneficial antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • (100 g) White rice parboiled
  • (100 g) Tomato sauce "Muti" brand
  • (70 g) Canned corn
  • (60 g) Canned kidney beans
  • (Single) Large egg
  • (To your liking) Turmeric, pepper, garlic powder, fenugreek seed
