Protein shake with chia seeds

To boost your performance

Shake of choice made from my favourite brand of meal replacements (yFood). It's made from lactose free whey protein and packs a lot of vitamins. I add chia seeds to boost absorption of fat-soluble vitamins and ground spices for a tasty twist.

Ingredients:

  • (250 ml) Oat milk
  • (4 scoops) yFood chocolate powder
  • (2 teaspoons) Raw chia seeds
  • (5 g) Creatine Creapure®
  • (To your liking) Ginger powder, Ceylon cinnamon powder, cardamom powder

Steps:

  1. Measure 250 ml of oat milk in a shaker.
  2. Add all the ingredients.
  3. Energetically shake it for 10 seconds. No need to use a blender.
  4. Pour it into a glass or drink directly from the shaker. Enjoy!
